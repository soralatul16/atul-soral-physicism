📘 MYP Physics Formula Bank

🔹 Forces & Energy

v = d / t   (Speed)
a = Δv / t   (Acceleration)
F = ma   (Force)
W = mg   (Weight)
W = Fd cos θ   (Work done)
P = W / t   (Power)
Efficiency = (useful output / input) × 100%
p = F / A   (Pressure)
GPE = mgh
KE = ½mv²

🔹 Electromagnetism

I = Q / t   (Current)
V = IR   (Ohm's Law)
P = IV   (Power)
E = VIt or Pt   (Energy)
(Vs / Vp) = (Ns / Np)   (Transformer)

🔹 Astrophysics

v = 2πr / T   (Orbital speed)

🔹 Heat, Light & Sound

Q = mcΔT   (Heat energy)
Q = mL   (Latent heat)
v = fλ   (Wave speed)

🔹 Waves

f = 1 / T   (Frequency)
n = sin i / sin r   (Refraction)

🔹 Atomic Physics

N = N₀(½)t / t₁/₂   (Half-life - advanced MYP)
