📐 Formula Quick-Reference ▼
F = ma
Newton's Second Law: Net Force = mass × acceleration
W = mg
Weight: Gravitational Pull = mass × gravitational field strength
P = F / A
Solid Pressure: Pressure = perpendicular force / contact area
a = (v - u) / t
Acceleration: Rate of change of velocity over time
v = u + at
Kinematic Equation: Final velocity under uniform acceleration
s = ½(u + v)t
Displacement: Distance covered under uniform acceleration
