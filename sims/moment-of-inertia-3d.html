The Physics

When an object rolls without slipping, its KE splits between translation and rotation:

KE = ½mv² + ½Iω²

Since v = ωr: KE = ½v²(m + I/r²)

The shape factor c = I/(mr²) determines how much energy goes to spinning vs moving. Lower c = faster down the ramp.

The Physics

L = Iω = constant (no external torque)

When the skater pulls arms in:
• r decreases → I decreases
• ω must increase to keep L constant

This is why figure skaters spin faster with arms tucked in.

Kepler's 2nd Law

Same principle: a planet moves faster when closer to the Sun (smaller r → smaller I → larger ω). Angular momentum is conserved because gravity exerts no torque about the Sun.
